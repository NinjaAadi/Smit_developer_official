Intro

This project was born from the desire for a closely knit and active coding culture at SMIT. Some of us friends and seniors came together to make this happen.

At SMIT DevBlocks we encourage participation and passion above all else. We welcome students of various levels of knowledege and experience since we strongly believe the best way to learn is to teach and collaborate. Here you can share your ideas, and learn new skills from your fellow students.

We hope you will fell at home. Good luck and Happy Coding :D
